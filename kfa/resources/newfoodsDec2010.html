New Allergy-Friendly Foods for Kids

Kids With Food Allergies traveled to historic Boston to attend Natural Products Expo East, the largest natural, organic and healthy products trade show on the East Coast. This year, the event attracted close to 20,000 industry members and more than 1,300 exhibits representing 96 countries.

We checked in with a significant number of manufacturers who exhibited at the Boston Convention & Exhibition Center and unearthed the latest allergy-friendly products to share with you. Read on to discover what new foods are available and where you can find them!

HomeFree™ Chocolate Chip Cookie Mini Packs

Company: HomeFree™ www.homefreetreats.com

Description: HomeFree's very popular 70% organic crunchy mini chocolate chip cookies now make an even easier snack on the go. The company just launched mini packs (1 oz.) of their mini chocolate chip cookies! Like all of their cookies, these are 100% whole grain, kosher pareve, and free of peanuts, tree nuts, eggs and dairy. The only soy is soy lecithin, and they contain no wheat, trans fat or cholesterol.




Free of these allergens:
Milk
Egg

 Wheat
Gluten
 Peanut
Tree nut
 Fish
Shellfish

 Soy

Cross-Contamination Issues: HomeFree™ states, “We produce our treats in our own dedicated facility free of peanuts, tree nuts, eggs and dairy. The only soy in the facility is lecithin, and there is no wheat, fish, shellfish or sesame. Further, we source our ingredients to be free from cross-contamination, and then additionally allergen test every batch and random cookie batches for traces of peanut, almond, egg and milk proteins, and we also test our gluten free products for traces of gluten.”

Allergen Labeling Policy: HomeFree™ always labels for allergens, and because they have their own dedicated facility, they can keep allergens fully out of the facility. They do not even allow them into the staff break room. The non-gluten free cookies do not use gluten free oats, and because of their high standards for the meaning of “free,” they choose not to label them as “wheat free.” However, there is no wheat in the facility.

Availability: Available at stores and institutions; see store locator. Stores include participating Whole Foods, Shaw's (Wild Harvest Section), Big Y, Central Market, Food Town, Giant Carlisle and Earth Fare. Also available at Homefreetreats.com.

Naked Juice offers 10-oz. sizes of the following juices: Berry Blast, Mighty Mango, Green Machine, O-J, Strawberry Banana and Probiotic Tropical Mango. Cross-Contamination Issues: A company representative states, “As a matter of quality standards, we always follow good manufacturing practices and strict procedures to avoid cross contamination on our share equipment when processing any of the eight major allergens. Please be aware that the following products contain one or more of the FDA's eight major allergens: Green Machine (wheat from wheatgrass) and Protein Zone (milk and soy). Protein Zone is not available in 10-oz. If you have a natural fruit or vegetable allergy, please be aware that all Naked Juice products are processed in a facility with multiple natural fruits and vegetables. Although our machines are thoroughly cleaned after each batch, we cannot guarantee that there will not be cross contamination on a molecular level.”

Allergen Labeling Policy: The company uses a “May Contain” statement that is listed on the package in the event a product is run on a line that also produces a product that contains a top eight allergen, since they cannot be 100% confident that the allergen would not carry over resulting in incidental exposure.

Creating Better Lives Today... One Child at a Time

Kids With Food Allergies is dedicated to creating better lives—today—for children with food allergies. As one of the largest organizations for families managing food allergies, KFA offers parents the latest information, helpful food and cooking advice, and a community of support to help families raise healthy, happy, well-nourished children. And because no parent should feel alone, KFA provides hope, comfort and friendship — 24 hours a day, seven days a week. 

If you are raising a child with food allergies, become a Family Member of Kids With Food Allergies today.
